Resume
I’m a Portland, Oregon-born Graphic Designer and Illustrator who grew up on the mean streets of Beaverton. I commuted to Portland until I was awarded a Bachelor of Arts degree in Art Practices with a minor in Graphic Design from Portland State University. During my educational career I explored Graphic Design, Web Design, Screen-printing, and Japanese. Thanks to the organization called Treehouse I was able to delve further into Website development. I frequently accumulate books from the library to satiate my thirst for knowledge in the realm of Design, Art, and intriguing ideas. I plan to pick up Screen-printing again in the future as a hobby to continue executing the concepts that are swimming in my head. My favorite color is purple.
