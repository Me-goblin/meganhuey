  • As an Image Artist at Nike, I organize the complex and abstract, presenting data visually in digestible formats. I develop an assortment of tools and visual guides for my team for collaboration and assisting in workflow.

    As a practiced Designer and Illustrator, I am ever inquisitive, gravitating towards new opportunities and creative outlets to learn and grow from—whether it’s laying out a multiple hundred-page planner using InDesign, finessing illustration line work in Illustrator, conceptualizing a sewing project on scraps of paper, or layering color for a screen-printed image. My works are often branded with a whimsical and colorful flair, an expression of my character.
