    Form Follows Function
    Poster

    The form something takes is informed by the way it will function. This is an important guideline to keep in mind when approaching design. In this poster, the phrase "Form Follows Function" is composed from an assemblage of geometric structures, referencing American architect Louis Sullivan, who coined the phrase.
    10 ½" W x 15 ½" H
